Drioca team

Drioca is an international team focusing on reference software development projects.
Our projects offer opportunity for developers to develop and demonstrate their skills in an online team of software developers.

Drioca projects

The projects are based on the following issues:

  • special base (or even business) logic to represent customers' requirement,
  • online presentation of the product,
  • public source codes,
  • functionalities are planned for demonstration purposes.

In 2016 we focus on beginner and junior level development tasks based on Java. Team members are supported also with coaching. New team members and professional mentors are welcome.

Current projects:

  • demo1

    • makes team members familiar with the development environment for teamwork (Java, JSF, github.com, heroku.com, RDB: PostgreSQL),
    • is to develop basic functions usable in other projects,
    • offers entry level independent development tasks for new team members.
      The project was launched and is in the initial phase.
      https://drioca-demo1.herokuapp.com/

  • trainer

    • base task is to develop a software for long distance runners to plan and register their training and results.
    • the solution is based on online data registration (JSF), data management and reporting (Java, SQL) with database stored data.
    • the application will be continuously developed from simple data registration to authenticated multidimensional planning and reporting software with special services.
      The project starts in August 2016.
